Our Process

How we secure your business

A proven four-step engagement process designed for clarity, speed, and maximum security impact.

01

Discovery & Scoping

We learn your environment, define the engagement scope, and set clear objectives and success criteria.

02

Testing & Analysis

Our experts conduct thorough testing — manual and automated — using industry-standard frameworks like OWASP, PTES, and MITRE ATT&CK.

03

Reporting & Briefing

You receive a detailed report with findings, business risk rating, and a remediation roadmap. We walk you through every finding.

04

Remediation & Retest

We validate your fixes with a free retest and provide ongoing guidance until all critical findings are resolved.
